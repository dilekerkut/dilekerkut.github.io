My background is in (language) teaching education, and in the past I have worked as SEO content writer for EWOR, as a freelance interpreter (English – Turkish – German), I have started a side project Vocabzine, an online dictionary (English – Turkish) via Instagram, and more recently I’m helping open source projects localize their content into Turkish. I have also been involved in international volunteer programs as interpreter.
